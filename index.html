Galaxy Builder

Form the galaxy the way you want

Our galaxy is something beautiful and complex. But have you ever had the desire to create your own? If so, you have the possibilty to do it right here, right now. Well not exactly a galaxy, but a solar system. Which is a good start, isn't it?

How to use

1. Select the amount of planets
2. Give your planets the needed attributes
3. Click 'render' and enjoy

The units are relative to earth. So for example 2 distance is twice the distance from the sun to earth.

Note: if you've already rendered at least once, you can click 'render last' to render your last input.
